About Fish Rig Finder

Fish Rig Finder is your AI-powered fishing assistant. Tell us what gear you have—rod, reel, line, hook size, lure or bait—and the conditions you’re facing. We’ll recommend the best fishing rig and setup for your target species so you spend less time guessing and more time catching.

Smart Fishing Rig Recommendations Based on Real Variables

Generic guides can be hit-or-miss. Fish Rig Finder adapts to your exact scenario: freshwater lakes and rivers, tidal creeks, inshore flats, surf, piers, and jetties. Whether you’re bottom fishing, float fishing, finesse jigging, or casting lures, our logic matches rigs, leaders, and terminal tackle to your equipment and water.

  • Optimizes rigs for bass, crappie, panfish, trout, striped bass, redfish, snook, and more.
  • Considers water clarity, depth, current, structure, and forage to refine choices.
  • Explains why a rig works and how to tie it, so you learn while you fish.
